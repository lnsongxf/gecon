Sample models

  • pure_exchange.gcn — Static pure exchange economy
  • rbc.gcn — Basic RBC model
  • rbc_ic.gcn — RBC with installation costs
  • rbc_cu.gcn — RBC with capacity utilization
  • rbc_hf.gcn — RBC with habit formation
  • rbc_mc.gcn — RBC with monopolistic competition
  • rbc_ts.gcn — RBC with two sectors
  • epstein_zin.gcn — Stochastic growth with Epstein-Zin preferences
  • home_production.gcn Home production (based on Benhabib J., Rogerson R. and Wright R. "Homework in macroeconomics: Household production and aggregate fluctuations." (1991))
  • ttb.gcn — Time-to-build model
  • tc_rs.gcn — Two countries with risk sharing
